Gunjan Saxena

The Kargil Girl

Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl is a 2020 Indian Hindi-language biographical
drama film directed by Sharan Sharma and produced under Dharma
Productions and Zee Studios. The film stars Janhvi as Indian Air Force pilot
Gunjan Saxena, Indian female air-force pilot one of the first women to fly in a
combat zone, alongside Pankaj Tripathi and Angad Bedi in supporting roles.
